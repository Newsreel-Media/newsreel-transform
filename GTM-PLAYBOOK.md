## 3. DEMO FLOW

### The Demo Article

Use a universally known article that's text-heavy and would clearly benefit from interactivity. Options:

- **Best option:** A recent AP or Reuters breaking news story (everyone knows it, no paywall)
- **Backup:** A well-known NYT or BBC feature that's publicly accessible
- **For college newspaper pitches:** Transform one of THEIR recent articles before the call

**Always transform the prospect's own article before reaching out.** Nothing sells like seeing your own work made better.

### 30-Second Pitch

> "Your articles deserve more than a wall of text. Newsreel Transform takes any article URL and turns it into an interactive story -- photos, quizzes, polls, animated stats -- in 60 seconds. No design skills, no developers. Same journalism, 3-5x the engagement. Polls alone get 52% more engagement than static content. We're free to try."

### 2-Minute Demo Script

```
[0:00-0:15] "Let me show you how this works. I'm going to grab
this article from [publication] -- it's about 1,200 words,
standard article layout."

[0:15-0:30] "I paste the URL into Transform... and hit go.
While it's working -- this takes about 60 seconds -- it's
reading the article, pulling key quotes, finding relevant
images, generating quiz questions, and building interactive
polls."

[0:30-1:00] "And here it is. Let me walk you through what
it built. Slide 1 -- hero image with the headline. Slide 2 --

not just scroll past. Slide 4 -- a poll. This is where it gets

[1:00-1:30] "The data shows interactive content gets 2-3x more
engagement than static. 25% of newspaper subscribers say

these on your site, share them on social, or drop them in
```

## 4. PRICING STRATEGY

### Competitive Landscape

| Tool | Pricing | Model |
|---|---|---|
| **Shorthand** | From $40/mo, enterprise custom | Per-seat, feature-gated |
| **StorifyMe** | Usage-based (MAU/page views) | Starter/Growth/Enterprise |
| **Google Web Stories** | Free (WordPress plugin) | Free but limited features |
| **Newspack** | Included in CMS subscription | Platform-bundled |

### Recommended Pricing

| Tier | Price | Limits | Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 10 transforms/month | Basic slides, Newsreel watermark, no embed, public only |
| **Pro** | $299/mo | 100 transforms/month | No watermark, embed code, custom branding, analytics, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | $999/mo | Unlimited transforms | API access, CMS integration, team seats, white-label, custom domain, priority |
| **Student Press** | $0 | 50 transforms/month | Pro features, no watermark, requires .edu email verification |

### Key gating decisions:

- **Free to Pro trigger:** Watermark removal + embed capability. Publishers won't embed content with someone else's brand on it.
- **Pro to Enterprise trigger:** API access + CMS integration + team seats. Newsrooms with multiple writers need this.
- **Usage-based vs. flat fee:** Start with flat fee (simpler to sell, predictable revenue). Move to usage-based only if you see power users doing 500+ transforms/month.
- **The student press tier is a growth hack.** Every student journalist who uses Transform in college will want it at their first job. This is the Figma playbook.

### Revenue projections (conservative):

- Month 1-3: 5 Pro accounts = $1,495/mo
- Month 4-6: 15 Pro + 1 Enterprise = $5,484/mo
- Month 7-12: 30 Pro + 3 Enterprise = $11,970/mo
- Year 1 target: ~$100K ARR (gets you to pre-seed close territory)

## 9. COMPETITIVE MOAT

**Why Transform wins vs. alternatives:**

| Competitor | What they do | Why Transform wins |
|---|---|---|
| **Shorthand** ($40+/mo) | Beautiful longform stories | Requires manual design work. Transform is instant (60 seconds vs. hours). |
| **StorifyMe** (usage-based) | Web Stories widgets | Focused on e-commerce. Not built for journalism. |
| **Google Web Stories** (free) | AMP-based visual stories | Google has deprioritized this. Limited features. No quizzes/polls. |
| **Canva** | Design tool | Not article-specific. Requires manual work. No auto-quiz/poll generation. |
| **Manual design** | In-house teams | Only large newsrooms can afford this. Transform democratizes it. |

**Transform's unique value:** Paste a URL, get interactive content in 60 seconds. No design skills. No developers. AI generates the quizzes, polls, and stats automatically. Nobody else does this.
